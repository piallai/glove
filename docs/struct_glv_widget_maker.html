Detailed Description

template<class Tvalue>
struct GlvWidgetMaker< Tvalue >

First layer of template specialization possibility. GlvWidgetMaker is also in charge of signals connection through GlvWidgetMakerConnect provided corresponding specialization is defined. GlvWidgetData must have get_value(), set_value() and set_editable() methods.
